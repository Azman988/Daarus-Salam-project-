Our Services

At Dar-Salam Islamic Medical Home, we provide a comprehensive range of healthcare services that integrate modern medical standards with the healing traditions of the Sunnah. Our core services are designed to address the physical, emotional, and spiritual well-being of our patients.

Prophetic Medicine (Tibb al-Nabawi)
  • Hijama (Wet Cupping Therapy): A traditional Sunnah practice performed in a clinical, sterile environment to aid detoxification, improve circulation, and relieve chronic pain.
  • Nutritional Therapy: Guidance on healing through natural foods mentioned in the Quran and Hadith, such as honey, dates, and black seed (Habbat al-Sauda), to restore bodily balance.
  • Herbal Consultation: Evidence-based use of traditional herbs for various ailments, ensuring all remedies are Tayyib (pure) and Shariah-compliant.
Spiritual & Mental Wellness
  • Islamic Counseling: Guidance and support based on Islamic principles to address emotional and spiritual concerns.
  • Ruqyah Services: Spiritual healing via the recitation of specific Quranic verses and authentic supplications (Du'as) for those seeking relief from spiritual or psychological distress.
  • Stress Management: Techniques and strategies to help manage stress and promote mental well-being within an Islamic framework.
General Clinical Care
  • Primary Care Consultations: Professional medical examinations and diagnosis for common ailments, delivered with a focus on patient modesty and dignity.
  • Maternal and Pediatric Care: Specialized healthcare services for mothers and children, ensuring their unique needs are met with compassion and expertise.
  • Preventive Care: Emphasis on health promotion and disease prevention through lifestyle modifications and regular screenings.
Support Services
  • Pharmacy: Easy access to prescription medications and over-the-counter treatments.
  • Home Based Care: Professional medical visits for patients who require treatment or recovery support within the comfort and privacy of their own homes.

know About Us

Dar-Salam Islamic Medical Home is a professional healthcare provider dedicated to integrating modern clinical excellence with the timeless ethical principles of Islam. We provide a holistic healing environment where medical expertise meets spiritual compassion, ensuring our patients receive care that respects their faith and physical well-being.

Our Mission:

We aim to provide high-quality, Shariah-compliant healthcare services that prioritize the sanctity of life and the dignity of the patient. We are driven by the Quranic principle that "to save a life is to save all of humanity," making every patient's recovery our primary objective.

Core Values:
  • Compassion: We treat every patient with empathy and kindness, recognizing the emotional and spiritual dimensions of healing.
  • Integrity: We adhere to the highest ethical standards in all aspects of our care, ensuring transparency and honesty in our interactions.
  • Excellence: We are committed to continuous improvement and innovation in medical practices to provide the best possible care and in accordance with the Sunnah.
  • Respect for Faith: We honor the religious beliefs of our patients, incorporating Islamic principles into our healthcare approach.
